Delicious Pasta Recipe 🍝

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes and stir well.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the pan and mix thoroughly.
  5. Season with salt, garnish with parsley, and sprinkle parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tip: Add grilled chicken or veggies for extra flavor.
